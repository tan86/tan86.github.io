Regular Expressions

  • . - Any character (except newline)
  • \d - digit (0-9)
  • \D - Not a digit
  • \w - Word Character (a-z, A-Z, 0-9, _)
  • \W - Not a Word Character
  • \s - Whitespace (space, tab, newline)
  • \S - Not a Whitespace

    • Anchors:

  • \b - Word Boundary (space and Beginning of line are word Boundary)
  • \B - Not a Word Boundary
  • ^ - Beginning of a Line
  • $ - End of a Line
  • [] - Matches Characters in Brackets
  • [^] - Matches Characters NOT in Brackets
  • | - Either or
  • () - Group

    • Quantifiers:

  • * - 0 or more
  • + - 1 or more
  • ? - 0 or one
  • {3} - Exact Number
  • {3,4} - Range of number (min, max)
